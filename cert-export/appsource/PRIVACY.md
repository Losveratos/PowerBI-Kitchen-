# Privacy Policy — ChartKitchen byDatenWG

_Last updated: 2026-07-13_

ChartKitchen byDatenWG is a Power BI custom visual. It processes the data you
bind to it **only inside the Power BI visual sandbox**, on your own machine or
in your own Power BI tenant, for the sole purpose of rendering the chart.

**The visual does not:**

- make any network requests;
- send, transmit, upload or share any data to the authors or any third party;
- embed or load any external code, scripts, fonts or trackers;
- read or write cookies, local storage, or any storage outside the standard
  Power BI property-persistence API (used only to remember your formatting and
  in-visual view choices within the report itself);
- collect analytics, telemetry, or personal information.

All data you visualize stays within your Power BI report and your Microsoft
tenant. The authors have no access to it.

The visual is provided free of charge under the Apache License 2.0, without
warranty of any kind.
